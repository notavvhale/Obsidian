# Promt

URGENT: EXACT POSE CONSTRAINTS — MUST FOLLOW VERBATIM.

Highly detailed 32mm tabletop miniature, standing stealth cleric paparazzi. MEDIUM_DETAIL (reduced ornamentation). ZBrush sculpt style, optimized for 3D printing, watertight mesh, clean topology.

CRITICAL POSE REQUIREMENTS (MANDATORY):
- THE MODEL MUST HAVE EXACTLY TWO ARMS. NO EXTRA LIMBS.
- LEFT HAND MUST GRIP THE TRIPOD STAFF (MIDPOINT GRIP).
- RIGHT HAND MUST HOLD THE CAMERA ONLY (ONE-HAND GRIP) AND THE FIGURE MUST BE LOOKING THROUGH THE VIEWFINDER.
- THE TRIPOD MUST NOT BE FREE-STANDING OR MERELY PLACED BESIDE THE FIGURE.

Explicit positional phrases to enforce:
"LEFT HAND GRIPS TRIPOD AT MIDPOINT, FINGERS WRAPPED AROUND SHAFT, THUMB VISIBLE."
"LEFT ELBOW SLIGHTLY BENT, LEFT LEG BEARING WEIGHT TOWARDS THE STAFF, WEIGHT DISTRIBUTION NATURAL."
"RIGHT ARM HOLDS CAMERA AND LOOKS THROUGH VIEWFINDER — CAMERA HELD ONLY BY RIGHT HAND."
"The right hand grips the camera body with the index finger on the shutter button."
"LEFT HAND NOT TOUCHING CAMERA."

Tripod description (core / reconciled):
- The tripod must be a FOLDED TRIPOD that can be opened — the generator must depict it as such (three legs that can spread).
- The tripod shall include a subtle staff-like shaft element (mild elongation) but remain primarily recognizable as a tripod, not a full staff.
- Minimal decoration: simple design, minimal runes, one cross motif near the head.

ADDITIONAL TRIPOD POSE & VISIBILITY REQUIREMENTS (MANDATORY):
- THE TRIPOD MUST BE OPENED AND HELD UPSIDE DOWN (THIS IS REQUIRED).
- THE THREE TRIPOD LEGS MUST BE SPREAD / SEPARATED AND POINTING UPWARD; ALL THREE LEGS MUST BE FULLY VISIBLE (NOT OVERLAPPING).
- THE CENTRAL SHAFT OF THE TRIPOD MUST POINT DOWNWARD WHILE THE LEGS POINT UPWARD.
- THE LEFT HAND MUST FIRMLY GRIP THE TRIPOD SHAFT AT ABOUT MIDPOINT; FINGERS MUST APPEAR WRAPPED AROUND THE SHAFT.
- THE CHARACTER IS HOLDING THE TRIPOD LIFTED ABOVE THE GROUND — THE TRIPOD MUST NOT TOUCH THE BASE OR THE GROUND.
- THE TRIPOD MUST NOT BE FREE-STANDING OR MERELY PLACED BESIDE THE FIGURE; it must be HELD AND CONTROLLED BY THE FIGURE.
- THE TRIPOD MUST HAVE A DISTINCT CROSS MOTIF OR SMALL CROSS ORNAMENT AT THE TOP OF THE SHAFT (near the grip/head area).
- THE TRIPOD LEGS MUST BE SPLAYED EVENLY WHEN HELD UPSIDE DOWN, AND NO LEG MAY BE OBSCURED BY THE FIGURE'S CLOAK, HAND, CAMERA, OR OTHER ACCESSORIES.
- ENSURE FRONT, SIDE AND BACK ORTHO VIEWS SHOW ALL THREE LEGS CLEARLY.

Tripod positional enforcement phrases (repeatable):
"LEFT HAND HOLDING AN OPEN TRIPOD UPSIDE DOWN"
"TRIPOD LEGS POINTING UPWARD"
"TRIPOD HELD ABOVE THE GROUND"
"TRIPOD NOT TOUCHING THE BASE"
"ALL THREE TRIPOD LEGS VISIBLE"
"TRIPOD LEGS SEPARATED AND DISTINCT"
"NO LEG OBSCURED BY CLOAK/HAND/CAMERA"
"LEGS SPLAYED EVENLY"
"TRIPOD HAS CROSS ORNAMENT AT TOP"

Camera:
- Large telephoto lens with single glowing crystal core. Camera body simplified. CAMERA MUST APPEAR OVERSIZED (TELEPHOTO) AND IS HELD ONLY BY THE RIGHT HAND.

Accessories:
- Hood down with black baseball cap with small silver cross on cap.
- Cloth mask covering mouth/neck.
- Simplified cleric elements: 1–2 crosses, max 3 pouches, one prayer book.
- Reduce dangling charms and micro-ornamentation to MEDIUM_DETAIL suitable for 3D printing.

RENDER & OUTPUT:
- Output: STL/OBJ, medium poly, clean topology.
